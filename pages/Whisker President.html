Whisker President

A game about helping a cat do its presidential duties while keeping distractions at bay.

About

Current Status: Working on it!

This is a project made in the UCSC CMPM 170 Capstone Series. It is a project collaborated on by 13 other people, whom I am proud to work with.

My thought process going into the project was: How can I contribute to a project whose premise is not my own creation? I wanted to be able to work from the sidelines, as up until this point all the games I had worked on were all based on ideas I helped come up with.
